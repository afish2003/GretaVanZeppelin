Welcome to our Music Comparison Project

This site is part of our final project for DIGIT 210, where we explore the musical similarities between Greta Van Fleet and Led Zeppelin. Our focus is on analyzing their use of chord progressions and lyrical themes to see how modern rock echoes the classic sound. Dive in to discover the connections, contrasts, and what makes each band unique!
